1. Eiffel Tower, Paris, France

    History: Completed in 1889, it’s an iconic symbol of France and a masterpiece of iron architecture.

    Location: Paris, France.

    Activities: Enjoyed the stunning view of Paris from the top, took photos at Champ de Mars, and learned about its fascinating history.

  2. Great Wall of China, China

    History: A series of fortifications built as early as the 7th century BC to protect Chinese states

    Location: Northern China.

    Activities: Walked along the ancient wall, enjoyed breathtaking views of the mountains, and explored the historical watchtowers.

  3. Statue of Liberty, New York, USA

    History: Gifted by France in 1886, it’s a universal symbol of freedom and democracy.

    Location: Liberty Island, New York, USA.

    Activities: Took a ferry ride to the island, learned about its historical significance, and captured scenic views of the NYC skyline.
