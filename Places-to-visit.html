1. Machu Picchu, Peru

    History: An Inca citadel built in the 15th century, known for its remarkable architecture and mystical surroundings.

    Location: Andes Mountains, Peru.

    Activities: Trek the Inca Trail, witness the sunrise, and explore the ancient ruins.

  2. Santorini, Greece

    History: Famous for its stunning sunsets, white-washed buildings, and historical significance as an ancient volcanic island.

    Location: Aegean Sea, Greece.

    Activities: Enjoy the beaches, explore the caldera, and savor Mediterranean cuisine.

  3. Pyramids of Giza, Egypt

    History: Built as tombs for the pharaohs around 2600 BC, these are among the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.

    Location: Giza Plateau, Egypt.

    Activities: Explore the pyramids, visit the Sphinx, and ride a camel in the desert. scenic views of the NYC skyline.
